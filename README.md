# Silent Wake

**Silent Wake** is a turn-based naval strategy game for iPhone and iPad.

Command the **Destroyer** or the **Submarine** in a tense duel of hidden orders, prediction, and tactical risk. Each round, players secretly program movement and firing commands, lock their orders, and watch the battle unfold.

Victory belongs to the commander who can read the opponent, control the board, conserve resources, and strike before being hunted down.

## Features

* Turn-based naval strategy gameplay
* Command either the Destroyer or the Submarine
* Hidden order programming
* Movement and firing decisions each round
* Play vs System mode
* Standard and Expert system skill levels
* Pass-and-play local multiplayer
* Online multiplayer through Apple Game Center
* Destroyer sonar tactics
* Submarine mobility
* Ammo, fuel, hit counts, victory, and draw conditions
* Portrait-first design for iPhone and iPad
* Atmospheric naval visuals and sound

## Game Modes

### Play vs System

Play against the system and choose between Standard and Expert skill levels.

### Pass-and-Play

Two players can play locally on the same device. Each player enters hidden orders before the round resolves.

### Online Multiplayer

Online play uses Apple Game Center for authentication, matchmaking, and multiplayer gameplay.

## Privacy

Silent Wake does not require a custom account or separate login.

Online multiplayer uses Apple Game Center. Game Center is provided by Apple and is used for player authentication, matchmaking, invitations, and online multiplayer gameplay.

Silent Wake does not use third-party advertising and does not track users across apps or websites.

# Silent Wake Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** June 8, 2026 Silent Wake respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Silent Wake handles information when you use the app. ## Information We Collect Silent Wake does not require a custom account and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, physical address, contacts, photos, precise location, or payment information. ## Game Center Silent Wake uses Apple Game Center to support online multiplayer features. When you use online multiplayer, Apple Game Center may be used for: * Player authentication * Matchmaking * Player invitations * Online multiplayer gameplay Game Center is provided by Apple. Information associated with your Apple Account and Game Center profile is handled by Apple according to Apple’s privacy practices and your device settings. Silent Wake does not receive or store your Apple Account password. ## Gameplay Data Silent Wake may store basic gameplay information locally on your device so the app can operate properly. This may include game settings, current match state, scores, sound preferences, or other gameplay-related information. This information is used only for app functionality and is not used to personally identify you. ## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking Silent Wake does not use third-party advertising. Silent Wake does not track you across apps or websites. Silent Wake does not sell personal information. Silent Wake does not use third-party analytics services. ## Children’s Privacy Silent Wake is a general-audience strategy game. Silent Wake does not knowingly collect personal information from children. ## Data Sharing Silent Wake does not sell, rent, or share personal information with advertisers or data brokers. Online multiplayer functionality is provided through Apple Game Center, and Game Center-related information is handled by Apple. ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. ## Contact For privacy questions, support, or feedback, please contact: [salvatoreangelone@gmail.com](mailto:salvatoreangelone@gmail.com)
